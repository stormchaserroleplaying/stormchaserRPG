# Air Elemental Spirit
*Medium Elemental, Unaligned*

### Defences
**STR:** 14, **DEX:** 12, **CON:** 13, **INT:** 7, **WIS:** 10, **CHA:** 13<br>

**Stamina:** 34

**Damage Reduction:**  1

**Damage Resistances:** electric and sonic

**Condition Immunities:** exhaustion, paralysed, petrified, poisoned, unconscious

### Ability Scores
**STR:** 18 (+4), **DEX:** 15 (+2), **CON:** 17 (+3), **INT:** 4 (-3), **WIS:** 10 (+0), **CHA:** 16 (+3)

**Speed:** 30 feet; fly 30 feet (hover)

### Actions
**Slam:** *Melee Weapon Attack:* 2d20+4 to hit; reach 5 feet; one target. Hit: 1d10 + 4 slashing damage

### Special Abilities
**Amorphous Form:** The elemental can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.
### Other
* [Darkvision](https://stormchaserroleplaying.com/stormchaserRPG/General/Awareness/Special/#darkvision) 60 feet
* Awareness 10 (1d20)
* Languages: Primordial, understands languages you speak
