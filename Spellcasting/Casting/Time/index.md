# Casting Time
Most spells require one or two actions to cast, but some spells require much more time to cast.

Some spells are triggered by an action on your turn. If you have multiple spells active (for example, two spiritual weapons), then a single casting of a spell may only be triggered once per round.

## Longer Casting Times
Certain spells (including spells cast as rituals) require more time to cast: minutes, or even hours. When you are attacked while casting, the attacker makes a check against the DC determined by your [Sustain a Spell](https://stormchaserroleplaying.com/stormchaserRPG/Spellcasting/Casting/Duration/#sustaining-spells/) action. On a hit, your casting is interrupted and mana spent is lost. If you want to try casting the spell again, you must start over and spend the mana again.
