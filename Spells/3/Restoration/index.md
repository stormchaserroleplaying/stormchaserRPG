# Restoration

## Aura of Vitality
*3rd level Restoration*<br>
**Casting Time:** 2 Actions<br>
**Range:** Self (30-foot sphere)<br>
**Components:** V<br>
**Duration:** Sustained, 1 minute

An aura radiates from you in a 30-foot sphere for the duration. When you create the aura and as 1 action while it persists, you can restore 2dR Stamina to one creature in it.

## Mass Healing Word
*3rd level Restoration*<br>
**Casting Time:** 1 Action<br>
**Range:** 60 feet<br>
**Components:** V<br>
**Duration:** Instantaneous

Up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range regain Stamina equal to 1dR plus your spellcasting ability modifier.

**At Higher Levels:** When you cast this spell using 4 or more mana, the healing increases by 1dR for each additional mana.
