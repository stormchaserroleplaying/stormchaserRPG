# Spells
This section describes spells in the Stormchaser RPG. It contains the arcane and divine spell lists, followed by spell descriptions, presented by spell level, school of magic and then in alphabetical order by the name of the spell.

## Purchasing Additional Spells
Sponataneous spellcasters, such as sorcerers and warlocks, can purchase additional spells known for an amount of CP equal to twice the spell's level.

| Spell Level | Character Point Cost |
|:-----------:|:--------------------:|
| 1st  | 2 |
| 2nd  | 4 |
| 3rd  | 6 |
| 4th  | 8 |
| 5th  | 10 |
| 6th  | 12 |
| 7th  | 14 |
| 8th  | 16 |
| 9th  | 18 |
| 10th | 20 |
