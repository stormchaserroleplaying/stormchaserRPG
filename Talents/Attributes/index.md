# Attribute Costs
The character point costs to increase attribute scores can be found below.

| Attribute | Point cost to advance to this value |
|:---------:|:-----------------------------------:|
| 6 | - |
| 7 | 5 |
| 8 | 5 |
| 9 | 2 |
| 10 | 2 |
| 11 | 1 |
| 12 | 2 |
| 13 | 2 |
| 14 | 5 |
| 15 | 5 |
| 16 | 10 |
| 17 | 10 |
| 18 | 15 |
| 19 | 15 |
| 20 | 20 |

For example, to advance from a score of 13 to a score of 14 costs 5 points. 

Your score may not exceed 20. Apply your species bonus to your attribute score only after spending points to increase it.
